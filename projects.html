Projects

Here’s a glimpse into some of the projects I've brought to life, spanning a diverse range of skills from UI/UX design and object-oriented programming to creatively directing Sunway's Got Talent.

Astronaut Runner

> View More

In my Object Oriented Programming Assignment, I have developed Astronaut Runner which is a thrilling 2D adventure game inspired by classics like Jetpack Joyride and Flappy Bird. In this game, players control an astronaut navigating through a field of meteors while collecting coins. The game features a unique pixelart UI, dynamic object generation, and customizable difficulty levels, ensuring a fresh and challenging experience each time. Developed with a focus on smooth and responsive gameplay, Astronaut Runner showcases my expertise in object-oriented programming and game design, offering both an engaging user experience and a visually distinct retro aesthetic.

SUCEMS

> View More

In my Software Engineering Assignment, I have developed Smart University Campus Energy Monitoring System (SUCEMS) which is an innovative solution designed to optimize electricity usage across university campuses. This project leverages smart meters, IoT sensors, and advanced data analytics to monitor and manage energy consumption in real-time. By providing actionable insights, automated alerts, and user-friendly controls through a mobile app, SUCEMS aims to reduce operational costs, promote sustainability, and support the university’s commitment to energy efficiency. Through this project, I learned how to effectively draw Software Engineering diagrams which enhanced my ability to visually represent complex system interactionsand also a hands-on experience in prototyping, allowing me to translate conceptual ideas into functional designs, ensuring a user-centered approach to system development.
